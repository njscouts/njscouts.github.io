2023 Events of Note!

  • April Scout Night at Citizen Bank Park April 21 at 7:05 PM is Scout Night! Tickets will be $20 per person, and we purchased through the pack so we can be in attendance together.

  • April 29th - Estelle Manor Park Group Camping! Scouts may hike, stay for a free dinner, or stay overnight! During the day, we will be working on requirements. Webelos will be going on a hike to fulfill their Webelos Walkabout. This will take the place of the April 25th Den Meeting.

  • Hook Arrow Shell Will be in May 12 - 14 for all scouts! Note: This is a correction, we previously had the wrong date of 10-12 sorry for the confusion

  • Summer We will likely go down to one pack meeting per month with plans for day and camping activities.

    • June is targeted to be a pack camping trip to Gettysburg (date still to be determined)
    • Egg Harbor City Lake Day Camp July 31 - August 4
    • Fossil Hunting (Day Activity)
    • Beach Day (Day Activity)
    • Crabbing in Somers Point (Day Activity)
    • Webelos Adventure Camp (July 5 - 8)
    • Family Camping! Most likely Blue Rocks Camp Ground (tentative August 26-27)

We'll also be conducting another poll to get thoughts and feelings about activities for the Spring. If you have any ideas, feel free to reach out.
