    2023 Old Events

    • Gettysburg camping trip June 16-18!

    • April Scout Night at Citizen Bank Park April 21 at 7:05 PM is Scout Night! Tickets will be $20 per person, and we purchased through the pack so we can be in attendance together.

    • April 29th - Estelle Manor Park Group Camping! Scouts may hike, stay for a free dinner, or stay overnight! During the day, we will be working on requirements. Webelos will be going on a hike to fulfill their Webelos Walkabout. This will take the place of the April 25th Den Meeting.

    • Hook Arrow Shell Will be in May 12 - 14 for all scouts! Note: This is a correction, we previously had the wrong date of 10-12 sorry for the confusion

    • Pinewood Derby RACE DAY! See more information here! This event is one of the funnest in cubscouts. Our event will be held at the municpal log building at the Gabriel Memorial Field in Galloway. We'll have some concessions available, and make a party out of it. On January 7th, we'll be doing a car cutting event. More details on that to come. We'll also be doing some on-the-side building sessions with materials for purchase (weights, colors, etc) and tips and advice during the den and pack meetings in preparation for the event. Weigh ins and side clinics will be available at the two den meetings prior January 24 and February 7. Please be ready to weigh your car in February 7 so we are not waiting to start the event. Ram Club and Wheels of Thunder Car club to come with their cars for a car show starting @ 4pm for the kids and parents to enjoy. They have been invited to stay for dinner & Races. Official check in time will be 6:00 PM, and we will have the race start between 6:30 PM and 7:00 PM. Please be as early as you can so we can wrap up awards by 8:00 PM. Thank you!

    • Blue and Gold Dinner The Blue and Gold is our opportunity to share a meal, recognize our leaders, and promote some of our scouts to new ranks. It will be a great time, and we hope that you can make it. Courtney will have more information, and we will update this site.

    • Webelos Group Cabin Trip This will be a fun one out of Avondale. We will be visiting Longwood Gardens, and doing all kinds of neat activities at the camp. Webelos and their siblings invited.
